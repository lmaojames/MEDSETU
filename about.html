About Us
MEDSETU was born out of a vision to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers through seamless real-time monitoring and centralized medical record management. Developed as a cornerstone project at COEP Technological University, MEDSETU started as an initiative to tackle two major healthcare challenges—accessibility to real-time health data and the fragmentation of medical records. With a strong foundation in Instrumentation and Control Engineering, IoT, and Data Science, this project integrates ECG monitoring, cloud-based data storage, and intuitive visualization to provide a comprehensive health tracking solution. What began as an academic project has the potential to revolutionize remote healthcare, telemedicine, and long-term patient monitoring. MEDSETU is more than just a system—it is a step toward a more connected, efficient, and patient-centric healthcare future.
