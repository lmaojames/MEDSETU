MEDSETU

1. What is MEDSETU?

MEDSETU is a platform designed to help users manage their health records and access medical documents conveniently in one place.

2. Who can use MEDSETU?

Anyone who needs a digital solution for health monitoring and document access, including patients, doctors, and healthcare professionals.

3. Is MEDSETU free to use?

Yes, MEDSETU offers free basic services. Additional features may require a subscription or one-time payment.

4. How do I create an account on MEDSETU?

Click on the "Login" button on the homepage and follow the sign-up instructions to create an account.

5. What health metrics can I track on MEDSETU?

MEDSETU allows you to monitor vital health parameters like blood pressure, sugar levels, heart rate, and more.

6. Can I sync data from wearable devices?

Currently, MEDSETU supports manual data entry. Future updates may include integration with wearable health devices.

7. What types of documents can I upload?

You can upload medical reports, prescriptions, lab test results, and other health-related documents in PDF, JPEG, and PNG formats.

8. How secure are my documents on MEDSETU?

Your documents are securely stored using encryption protocols to ensure maximum privacy and security.

9. Can I share my documents with doctors?

Yes, MEDSETU allows you to securely share your health records with doctors via a link or direct access.

10. I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

11. I uploaded the wrong document. How can I delete it?

Go to the "Upload Documents" section, find the document, and use the delete option to remove it.

11. Why am I unable to upload my documents?

Ensure your file format is supported (PDF, JPEG, PNG) and that it does not exceed the maximum file size limit. Privacy & Security
